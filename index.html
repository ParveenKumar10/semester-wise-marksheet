Academic Formulas Used

  • SGPA (Semester GPA) = Σ(Credit × Grade Point) ÷ Σ(Total Credits in that semester)
  • CGPA = Σ(SGPAi × Creditsi) ÷ Σ(Creditsi)
  • Percentage = CGPA × 9.5
  • Result: Pass if SGPA ≥ 5 and no F grade; otherwise Fail
